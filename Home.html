Da Nang-The most livable coastal city

As the largest city in the Central region, Da Nang embraces the bustle and hustle of the region's. Surrounded by the blue sea and mountains, this is a city worth living with many captivating landscapes and unique cultural values. The simple and kindly people of Da Nang have become the symbol of this land.

Famous Dishes

Mi Quang is a famous Vietnamese dish originating from the Quang Nam province. It features thick yellow rice noodles in a flavorful broth made from pork bones and shrimp. Toppings such as shrimp, pork, herbs, peanuts, and rice crackers are added for a delicious combination of flavors and textures. Unlike other noodle soups, Mi Quang is served with a moderate amount of broth, allowing the ingredients to shine. It is a must-try dish when visiting Da Nang and the central region of Vietnam.

Ba Na Hill

Ba Na Hill in Da Nang, Vietnam is a captivating destination known for its stunning landscapes, French-inspired architecture, and iconic Golden Bridge. With thrilling attractions, beautiful gardens, and a cool climate, it offers a memorable experience for visitors seeking a refreshing escape. Ba Na Hill combines natural beauty, cultural influences, and modern entertainment, making it a must-visit destination in Da Nang.

Thanh Khe beach

Thanh Khe Beach in Da Nang, Vietnam is a serene coastal destination known for its pristine sandy shores and tranquil atmosphere. With its clean environment and beautiful views, it offers a peaceful retreat away from the crowds. Visitors can relax on the white sand, enjoy water sports, and savor local cuisine at beachside cafes, making it a favored spot for a serene beach experience.
